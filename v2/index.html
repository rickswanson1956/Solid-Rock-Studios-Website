The Artist

Built by hand. Made to last.

Dixon is a Rogers, Minnesota artist with a singular obsession: turning raw natural materials into permanent, personal art. What started as a passion for stone has grown into a studio that works across three mediums — stone, vinyl, and wood — each laser-engraved with precision and finished with care.

Every piece begins with a conversation. Dixon works directly with each customer — no middleman, no templates — to design something that carries meaning. The result isn't decor. It's an heirloom.

Based in the Twin Cities metro, Solid Rock Studios ships across the country. Prices range from $25 to $200. Most pieces take one to two weeks from design approval to your door.
